Analyzing the Influence of the Budget

Inferring from the above graphs, it can be comprehended that the views of people from various sectors, before the interim budget, was in favor of congress due to many parameters which included the elections held in various states and the loan waiver scheme. Modi sold voters a fabulous dream, promising good governance and a dynamic economy that would create jobs for all. In reality, Modi has failed to accelerate GDP growth or create the promised jobs for all. Recent state elections reveal voters in a sour anti-incumbent mood, and in 2019, the NDA will be the incumbent. It has lost two allies, the Telugu Desam Party (TDP) and Shiv Sena.

The new budget of 2019 was designed in keeping in mind the votebank in which the major part was occupied by the middle class people. They were given several respites in this budget. But according to the graphs it seems that people are still not satiated with the government. The negative views for BJP have increased than before. Along with this, congress has lost few supporters as well but the loss was less. It seemed that BJP didnt win many hearts in the brief tenure of 5 years thus it can be inferred that #AbkiBaarModiSarkaar may change to #AbkiBaar400Paar.
